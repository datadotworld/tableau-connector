Start Using Tableau

With Tableau, you can easily create beautiful visualizations from your data and share with a click.

Download Tableau

Create an account and download either Tableau or Tableau Public v.10+
Note: Tableau Public is free to use

Connect to data.world

Using the web data connector, you can connect Tableau to your data.world account

  1. Choose More from the Server menu, then select Web Data Connector
  2. Enter tableau.data.world in the text field and hit Enter to submit
  3. Once you login & authorize data.world, enter the URL for the desired project, or search available projects with the Search feature

Select Data & Create Visualization

Once you're connected to a data.world project, you can create visualizations from your data:

  1. Drag your selected table to the main panel, then navigate to your worksheet by selecting Sheet 1 from the bottom bar
  2. Drag & Drop Dimensions & Measures to your visualization and select your visualization type.

    Tip: You can manually edit visualization settings like color, chart type, size, label, and more through the Marks panel to the left of your visualization

Share Your Visualization

Using Tableau Online, you can easily and quickly share your visualization:

  1. Click the Share icon on the top bar and complete any requested fields, then Publish!
  2. Note: If you don't already have a Tableau Online account, you'll need to register for one before sharing.
